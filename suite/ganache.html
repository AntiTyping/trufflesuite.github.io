THE SWEET FEATURES AT THE CORE OF GANACHE

THE MENU

  1. THE ETHEREUM BLOCKCHAIN

    GANACHE ships with an internal Javascript implementation of the Ethereum Blockchain which has additional programmatic capabilities - no local clients need to be installed!

  2. VISUAL MNEMONIC & ACCOUNT INFO

    Quickly see the current status of the generated accounts, including their addresses, private keys, nonces and balances.

  3. BLOCKCHAIN LOG OUTPUT

    See the output of web3 calls to the blockchain, along with responses and other vital debugging information.

  4. INSTANT MINING CONTROLS

    Stop, start, force mine a single block, and take or revert multiple snapshots of the blockchain as and when you chose.

  5. BUILT-IN BLOCK EXPLORER

    Examine specific blocks or transactions at will and see detailed information about the parameters.

  6. BLOCKCHAIN CONNECTED CONSOLE

    Access a Node.js powered Console to execute commands, with a fully initialized instance of web3, and many useful commands to make development and testing easy.
